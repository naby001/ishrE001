aQuest

Released: June 12, 2013.
This is a national level Quiz competition, related to HVAC&R. This competition is held is several rounds. The first stage is in December, in regional level.

Student Research Project Grant (SRPG)

Released: September 11, 2013.
This competition is more of a research project. A few core ideas are given, and students are asked to develop products out of it. SRPG provides students with funding, mentorship, and resources to help them conduct research on topics related to HVAC&R.

National Students Design Competition

Released: February 12, 2014.
This competition asks students to develop HVAC system of some corporate buildings, sometimes it is shopping mall, sometimes office etc. It provides students with the opportunity to learn and work on a practical project and develop new skills.

Hackathon

Released: August 19, 2014.
Students look to brainstorm ideas and develop innovative solutions on pre-decided problem sets. The problem changes every year. Last year it was based on use of solar energy in refrigeration. .

ENTROPY

Release Date: April 29, 2015
It initiated a narrative series for BTS and explored the complexities of youth. The album included tracks like "I Need U" and "Dope."

REFCOLD-HACKATHON

Released: November 30, 2015
ISHRAE's first event in this calendar year, The Refcold-23 Hackathon is a student-led event that aims to promote innovation in the field of HVAC&R to solve agricultural problems. Winners get incubation benefits of Rs 1,00,000. held in June-july (tentative)

Solar Decathlon

Released: May 2, 2016.
Solar Decathlon India is an annual, 9-month long challenge for undergraduate and postgraduate students from India to combat Climate Change by innovating and designing net-zero energy and climate resilient building solutions.

RACON

Released: October 10, 2016.
This competition encourages students to become involved in a profession that is crucial for ensuring a sustainable future for our Earth. By participating in this Competition Students will get an opportunity to extend their knowledge beyond their Engineering Course Curriculum by actually designing a System.
